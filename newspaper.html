10 of the most advanced military robots in the world

Advantages of military robots:

  • They keep (our) humans out of harm’s way: Robots handle risky jobs like bomb disposal, scouting enemy positions, and even combat — reducing the danger for some soldiers.
  • They improve operational efficiency: Robots can gather intel, process data, and perform their duties at lightning speed. Plus, they don’t need time off, get stressed, tired, or absent-minded during life-or-death scenarios.
  • They save human resources: Overall, robots cut down the workforce on heavy-duty tasks like logistics and surveillance. Soldiers can re-focus on combat-centric or intel tasks.
  • Affordable and adaptable: RO1 costs half the purchase price of comparable robots for lease, starting at $5/hour.
  • Precision and power: With an 18 kg payload and repeatability of ±0.025 mm, RO1 handles everything from assembly to welding without blinking.
  • AI and no-code simplicity: Featuring AI capabilities on par with GPT-4 and a no-code setup, RO1 is easy to program — no robotics know-how needed.
  • Safety-first design: Equipped with machine vision and collision detection, RO1 integrates perfectly alongside human teams without any safety barriers.
